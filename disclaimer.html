Statistical insights & probability scores

The probability percentages and context-aware insights shown throughout Criclytics are generated by a machine learning model trained on historical cricket data. These figures represent how often similar situations resolved a certain way in the past. They are:

  • Not predictions of what will happen in any future match or innings.
  • Not expert opinions, tips, or recommendations of any kind.
  • Not guaranteed to reflect current team form, player fitness, pitch conditions, or any other live variable.

For example: a "62% win probability" means that, historically, teams in comparable match situations won approximately 62% of the time. It does not mean the team will win today.

Every probability figure on the platform is labelled as a historical estimate and accompanied by this disclaimer context.
