🏏 About Criclytics

Context-aware cricket analytics that goes beyond scorecards — turning historical data into meaningful, probabilistic insight.

What we built

Criclytics is a cricket analytics platform built for fans, analysts, and enthusiasts who want more than career averages. Every stat on this platform carries context — the venue, the opponent, the format — because a batsman averaging 45 across all grounds tells a very different story when that average drops to 18 against a specific pace attack on a specific pitch.

The platform covers all three major international formats — T20I, ODI, and Test — with dedicated sections for players, teams, venues, matches, rankings, and records, tied together by a probability engine trained on historical ball-by-ball data.

Our philosophy

Cricket is a game of context. A score of 150 in a T20I chasing at a high-scoring venue is a very different achievement from 150 in a Test innings opener on a green pitch. Criclytics is designed around this belief — every number on the platform is surfaced with its context attached.

All probability insights are statistical observations from historical data, never predictions. We built this platform to make advanced cricket intelligence accessible to everyone, not just data scientists.

Important to know

Criclytics is not a prediction or betting platform. Every probability figure on this site is a historical frequency estimate — it reflects how often similar situations ended a certain way in the past. It is not a forecast of any future match or performance. See our full disclaimer for details.
